About BusinessPlan.AI

Founded in 2022 by a team of successful entrepreneurs and AI experts, BusinessPlan.AI emerged from a simple observation: starting a business shouldn't be complicated. Our founders, having built and sold multiple seven-figure companies, wanted to democratize access to professional business planning tools and expertise.

Our mission is to empower one million entrepreneurs to launch successful businesses by 2025. We believe in innovation, accessibility, and data-driven decision making. Every feature of our platform is designed to turn complex business planning into a simple, intuitive process that anyone can complete in minutes.
