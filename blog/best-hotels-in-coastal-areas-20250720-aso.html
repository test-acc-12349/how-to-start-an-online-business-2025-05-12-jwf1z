Best Hotels in Coastal Areas

When it comes to planning a relaxing getaway, few experiences compare to staying at one of the best hotels in coastal areas. Whether you're looking for luxury, breathtaking ocean views, or easy beach access, coastal hotels offer an unparalleled escape from the everyday.

Luxury Coastal Retreats

1. The Ritz-Carlton, Laguna Niguel (California, USA)

Perched atop a stunning bluff overlooking the Pacific Ocean, The Ritz-Carlton in Laguna Niguel is the epitome of coastal luxury. This five-star hotel boasts elegant rooms with private balconies, a world-class spa, and direct access to Salt Creek Beach.

2. Four Seasons Resort Seychelles

Nestled on a hillside overlooking Petite Anse Bay, the Four Seasons Resort Seychelles offers a secluded paradise. The hotel features private villas with infinity pools, white sandy beaches, and exceptional service.

Boutique Coastal Gems

3. Belmond Hotel das Cataratas (Brazil)

Located within Brazil's Iguazu National Park, this boutique hotel offers a unique coastal-like experience near the breathtaking Iguazu Falls. The colonial-style architecture and lush surroundings make it a standout choice.

4. The Dylan (Amsterdam, Netherlands)

While not directly on the coast, The Dylan offers a charming riverside escape with easy access to the North Sea. This boutique hotel combines historic charm with modern amenities.

Family-Friendly Coastal Hotels

5. Atlantis Paradise Island (Bahamas)

A dream destination for families, Atlantis Paradise Island features expansive water parks, marine exhibits, and pristine beaches. The hotel offers accommodations for all budgets.

6. Beaches Turks & Caicos

This all-inclusive resort is perfect for families, offering kid-friendly activities, gourmet dining, and endless water sports.

Key Features to Look For

  • Ocean views and beach access
  • Quality of amenities
  • Family-friendly facilities
  • Dining options
  • Spa and wellness services
  • Location and accessibility
  • Value for money
